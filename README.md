## A bit about me

I'm a cybersecurity engineer with over **6 years** working in **SOC and Blue Team** environments — across three countries and two continents. I've configured SOAR platforms for telecom giants in Sweden, built threat intelligence systems from scratch at Reliance Industries, and now work as an **IT Security DevOps Engineer at epay (a Euronet company)** in Munich.

What drives me isn't just threat-hunting or hardening systems — it's the puzzle. The moment a noisy alert resolves into a real incident, or when an anomaly turns out to be exactly what you suspected. I find that deeply satisfying.

Outside of my day job, I'm building **Flexorajobs** — a gig workforce marketplace for hospitality and logistics workers in India. It's what I work on when I want to *create* rather than *defend*.

I studied **Cybersecurity (MTech)** at Amrita University and later did an **MSc in UX Design** at Jönköping University in Sweden — because understanding *how humans behave* makes you a far better security engineer.

> Client-side work for a major telecom company based in Kista.

---

**`Aug 2019 → Aug 2022`** &nbsp;|&nbsp; 🏢 **Reliance Industries Ltd.** — *Cyber Security Engineer* &nbsp;|&nbsp; Mumbai 🇮🇳

Four distinct things I built here that I'm genuinely proud of:

1. **Threat Intelligence Platform** — Open-source TIP built from scratch. Connected to TAXII feeds, enriched live ingested data in real time, and piped threat data to business vendors.
2. **Automated Penetration System** — Modules covering SQLi, XSS, directory traversal, SSL vulnerabilities, firewall alertness, and URL deny-listing. On-demand and scheduled scans via Burp Suite. Impersonated threat actors to stress-test real resilience.
3. **CTF Platform for Recruitment** — Designed a Capture the Flag system for hiring. Challenges in web app pentesting, forensics, cryptography. Used to identify real talent through real scenarios — not just resumes.
4. **Adversarial Phishing Campaigns** — Company-wide phishing simulations with a custom scoring system to flag who needed further training. Built security culture from the inside out.
